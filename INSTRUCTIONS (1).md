## Instructions

### App Planning & Requirements Gathering
Clarify the core purpose and audience before building.

- **What is your app called?**
  Smoking App

- **What core problem does it solve, and for whom?**
  The app solves the problem of not having a simple way to find and organize smoking places, and to connect with others who share similar interests.

- **Can you describe the key workflow or the "main magic" moment for users?**
  The main magic moment for users is when they can easily find and save smoking places, connect with others, and access a community forum and resources for smoking-related topics.


### Essential Features
- **List the must-have features the app requires for a useful MVP.**
  Must-have features include a map view to find smoking places, a gallery to save favorite spots, a community forum, and integration with social media platforms.

- **Which features are nice-to-have or for future versions?**
  Future features could include AI-powered recommendations for smoking places, a review system for spots, and partnerships with smoking accessory retailers.


### User Experience
- **What are the main user roles?**
  The main user roles are smokers who want to find and connect with others, and retailers who want to promote their products and services.

- **What should each user be able to do?**
  Users can view and save smoking places, connect with others, access the community forum, and purchase smoking-related products and accessories.

- **Are there any access restrictions?**
  There are no access restrictions for viewing the app's content, but users must be 18+ to create an account and participate in the community forum.


### Integrations & Notifications
- **Should the app integrate with external APIs or services?**
  The app should integrate with social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram, as well as Google Maps for location services.

- **For the MVP, do you want real integrations or simulated flows?**
  The app will use real integrations with external APIs and services.

- **Should users receive notifications? If yes, how often and for what?**
  Users will receive notifications for new messages, comments, and updates from the community forum, as well as promotional offers from retailers.


### Data, Security & Privacy
- **What core data or objects are stored?**
  The core data stored includes user profiles, smoking place locations, community forum posts, and product information from retailers.

- **Should users have privacy settings or profiles?**
  Users have control over their profile information and can choose to share their location and other data with the community.

- **Any GDPR or compliance needs?**
  The app will comply with relevant laws and regulations, including GDPR and age restrictions for tobacco products.


### Special Features
- **Should users be able to upload media?**
  Users can upload photos and reviews of smoking places, as well as their own products and accessories.

- **Any AI or automation features?**
  The app will use AI-powered recommendations to suggest smoking places and products to users based on their preferences and behavior.

- **Any unique requirements?**
  The app will have a unique feature that allows users to connect with others who share similar interests and preferences.


### Design & Branding
- **Any specific design vision, brand values, colors, or style?**
  The design vision is a modern, sleek, and user-friendly interface that incorporates elements of smoking culture and community.

- **Should the app support dark/light mode?**
  The app will have a dark theme by default, with a toggle option for a light theme.
